1. download kernel source
2. extract kernel source
3. `make menuconfig` or copy old config and `make oldconfig`
4. `make`
5. copy ./arch/x86_64/boot/bzImage to /boot/vmlinuz-*
6. copy ./System.map to /boot/System.map-*
7. `make modules_install`
8. `dracut --force`
9. edit /boot/EFI/refind/refind.conf
10. `xbps-reconfigure -f virtualbox-ose-dkms`
11. cleanup in /boot, /lib/modules, /var/lib/dkms/virtualbox-ose, /usr/src
