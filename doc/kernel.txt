 1. get kernel source
 2. `make oldconfig`, `make menuconfig`
 3. `make [CC=clang HOSTCC=clang]`
 4. copy ./arch/x86_64/boot/bzImage to esp/EFI/linux/vmlinuz-VER
 5. copy ./System.map to esp/EFI/linux/System.map-VER
 6. `make [CC=clang HOSTCC=clang] modules_install`
 7. `dracut -o "btrfs crypt dm lvm lunmask mdraid" esp/EFI/linux/initramfs-VER.img VER`
 8. update esp/EFI/linux/refind_linux.conf
  - `mkrlconf`
  - intel_iommu=igfx_off (disable iommu for gpu)
  - intel_pstate=passive (enable schedutil cpu gov)
 9. `xbps-reconfigure -f virtualbox-ose-dkms`
  - for clang build: link /usr/bin/[g]cc to clang
l0. cleanup in /boot, /lib/modules, /usr/src, /var/lib/dkms/virtualbox-ose
